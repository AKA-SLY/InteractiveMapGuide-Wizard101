Sorcerer's Portent
Spell Information
School
Pip Cost4
Accuracy100%
Type / 
Spell Description
Deals 200 Balance Damage to and applies a 100 Heal Absorb on enemy
Training Sources
This Spell cannot be trained
This Spell is to available to the Balance Krokomummy Beastmoon Form during Beastmoon events.


Beastmoon Form Caster
  • Balance Krokomummy
